Amrutvahini College of Engineering (AVCOE), located in Sangamner, is a prominent autonomous engineering institute established in 1983. The college is accredited with an 'A+' Grade by NAAC and has been accredited four times by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA). It is permanently affiliated with the Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU).
